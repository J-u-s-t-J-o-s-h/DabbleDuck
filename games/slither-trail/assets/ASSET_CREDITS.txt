Slither Trail — Asset Credits
=============================

Visuals
-------
All in-game 3D art (the snake, collectibles, garden decorations, fences, trees,
butterflies, fireflies) is generated procedurally in GDScript from simple meshes
(spheres, boxes, cylinders). No external 3D model files are shipped. This keeps
the download tiny and guarantees the game runs on the GL Compatibility renderer
used for older Dell PCs and budget mini PCs.

Audio
-----
- sparkle.ogg, win.ogg — Kenney "Interface Sounds" pack (CC0 / public domain).
  Source: https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds
  License: see licenses/kenney-interface-sounds-license.txt
- Background music, collection blip, gentle "oops", and bird chirps are
  synthesized procedurally at runtime (see scripts/game_audio.gd). If a CC0 OGG
  is present it is used; otherwise the procedural fallback plays, so the game
  always has sound.

Approved CC0 / open sources for future art drops (see docs/games/slither-trail-design.md):
- Kenney            https://kenney.nl              (CC0)
- Quaternius        https://quaternius.com         (CC0)
- Poly Pizza        https://poly.pizza             (varied, attribute per model)
- OpenGameArt       https://opengameart.org        (varied, attribute per asset)
- Godot Asset Lib   https://godotengine.org/asset-library
- itch.io free packs (varied, attribute per pack)
