Classic Snake — Asset Credits
=============================

Visuals
-------
All 3D art (snake, apple, board, fence) is generated procedurally in GDScript
from simple meshes. No external model files are shipped. Runs on the GL
Compatibility renderer for older Dell PCs and budget mini PCs.

Audio
-----
- sparkle.ogg, win.ogg — Kenney "Interface Sounds" pack (CC0 / public domain).
  Source: https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds
  License: see licenses/kenney-interface-sounds-license.txt
- Background music and the gentle "oops" are synthesized procedurally at runtime
  (scripts/game_audio.gd), with the CC0 sparkle used as the "eat" sound. If a CC0
  OGG is missing, a procedural fallback plays so there is always sound.
